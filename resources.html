Find the people in your organization with the knowledge to get stuff done

Earn more by improving your productivity r/ally helps to increase your productivity by finding the right people in your organization to help you with your work. Simply Post a Goal in the r/ally app to your colleagues, to find out who knows about the topic you need information on. r/ally’s natural language search engine finds fellow team members with the most relevant skills and experience. It notifies them that you need assistance and lists the related topic. You can then have a private one-to-one conversation in r/ally with your colleague to help you get the information you need. For those who offer support and provide it, you can give them Kudos, which results in your colleague’s expertise being recognized by r/ally. You will also get recognition and a more favorable weighting for that skill in future r/ally searches.

Be Known

Raise your profile by showcasing your expertise

When you offer to support colleagues who need help, it builds your expertise in the topic you help them with. As a result, you will be recognized as someone who has skills and experiences in your subject matter. The more you help, the higher your profile becomes for those skills sets. As you work on different projects and accumulate more skills, r/ally increases your rating for those skills and as relevant skills are requested you appear higher up the list of qualified team members for that next big project.

Be Happy

Get staffed on projects that improve your skills

r/ally’s enhanced analytics coupled with its machine learning capabilities, enables team leaders, project managers and talent managers to build project teams comprised of individuals with the most relevant skills for the project. Using the Talent Management solution, which is part of the r/ally Data Analytics package, type in a natural language request, for example, “Find someone who has marketing skills, knowledge of presentations and experience with Keynote who knows Spanish” r/ally returns a list of those individuals with skills that match your request and displays the information it used to qualify its response. The more exact matches appear at the top of the list, with those who have more loosely related skills and knowledge further down the list of responses. By keeping your skills up to date, r/ally will recognize you as being most suitably qualified for the project. It doesn’t matter if there are 100 or 100,000 people in your enterprise, r/ally will find your skills if they are relevant to the skills, experience or knowledge of the project.
