Add Intelligent Automation to Your HR/HCM Solutions

Over the last 20 years, HR/HCM software packages have automated the HR process. They help HR/HCM and Talent Managers to be more productive by automating the HR process from recruitment, to onboarding, learning and performance management. r/ally is a dynamic company that adds takes HR software to the next level. r/ally introduces intelligence to automation. Our unique, machine learning search engine leverages your own data to save significant time and effort looking for human intellectual property by uncovering the hidden skills, project experience, product knowledge and other valuable information in your workforce; skills and knowledge you won’t find using traditional search tools. r/ally makes everyone in your organization more productive by discovering hidden skills, building knowledge teams, identifying the MVPs with the most valuable and rare skills, and crucially, augmenting your skills ‘weak spots’ in the knowledge base of today’s mobile, transient, geographically dispersed organizations.

r/ally’s Human Capital Search

Discover Who Knows What In Your Organization

Knowledge workers spend up to 60% of their time searching for information that someone in your enterprise already knows r/ally is a unique machine learning enterprise search solution that allows you to search for individuals with the relevant skills across your entire enterprise. r/ally gets answers to questions that will help you and co-workers to complete everyday tasks. You enter the skills, knowledge or experience you are looking for in natural language form and r/ally’s search engine provides a ranked list of candidates who it thinks can help you. The unique aspect of r/ally is that it not only understands skills; it uses continuously updated external data sources to understand associated concepts across multiple industry segments. So, industry specific terms or products, r/ally understands the association between skills, experience, knowledge, products and everything related to your team members work activities and history. Using ‘traditional’ keyword or Boolean searches, these skills are hidden unless the exact word (spelled correctly) appears in someone’s profile.

r/ally Explorer – Adding Intelligence to the ATS for Staffing and Recruitment Professionals

By 2020 more than 40% of the workforce will be a temporary worker, contractor, or someone who will plan to be employed by more than one enterprise during each calendar year. This presents a unique challenge for Staffing and Recruitment Professionals – both internally in the enterprise and externally through independent staffing and recruitment services companies. Searching databases of thousands, and in some cases millions, of resumes to find the right individual quickly and efficiently is a labor-intensive task, particularly because today’s requirements from employers are for very specific skills and experience. r/ally Explorer applies machine learning algorithms to our enterprise search solution to enable search consultants to find individuals already hidden in their existing ATS or legacy databases. Because of its knowledge of associated concepts, r/ally finds people with associated skills, knowledge or experience in your resume system that are closely connected to the skills you need, even if those skills are not specifically listed in your database. And because r/ally is not another ATS or database system, it’s non-invasive. Once it’s connected to your ATS, it will automatically search updated profiles. r/ally for Semiconductor, Aerospace & Technology Industries

Some of the products in electronics and manufacturing are designed to outlive the careers of the people who designed and created them. It’s not unusual for an integrated circuit or an avionics component to have a life span of decades. Likewise the applications developed for large-scale enterprise IT projects also have a life span of many years and require continual maintenance. Many financial services companies still use IBM mainframes applications written in the 1980s and 1990s. One of the biggest risks to these enterprises is that the developers and designers of these products retire or leave to work for a competitor. r/ally’s Human Capital Strategic Planning enables enterprises to assess its skills ‘inventory’ and to determine at what point in time certain skills may be in short supply, and to augment skills or experience or rebalance skills across the organization to ensure that customer requirements, Service Level Agreements are met, or that the product roadmap ids not jeopardized by because the lead designer retires or leaves for a competitor.
