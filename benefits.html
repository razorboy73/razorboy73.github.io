Generate Significant Cost Savings for your Organization

In a typical enterprise, employees spend up to 60% of their time looking for information. This includes time spent emailing colleagues, making phone calls and even using an internal intranet or wiki to find expertise.

However, unless there is an exact keyword match on what you need help with, traditional knowledge searches don’t help. r/ally’s machine learning algorithms understand related concepts and can find someone with a skill that is a loosely connected subject matter to the skill, experience or knowledge set that you are looking for.

If every user made use of r/ally just twice a week, an enterprise with 300 users would save over $1 million per year in time recovered from wasted searches by traditional methods such as email, phone calls, and even traditional keyword searches which are not effective if the exact keyword is not found.

Raise your profile by showcasing your expertise

When you offer to support colleagues who need help, it builds your expertise in the topic you help them with. As a result, you will be recognized as someone who has skills and experiences in your subject matter. The more you help, the higher your profile becomes for those skills sets.

As you work on different projects and accumulate more skills, r/ally increases your rating for those skills and as relevant skills are requested you appear higher up the list of qualified team members for that next big project.
